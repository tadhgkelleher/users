Mandalas in Sci-Fi and Fantasy

Ever notice mandala-like patterns in sci-fi films? Think of the swirling galaxy maps in *Star Wars* or the intricate portals in *Doctor Strange*—they echo mandala designs, blending cosmic wonder with storytelling. These aren’t just circles; they’re gateways to other worlds.

Digital and Fractal Mandalas

In the digital age, artists use code to create mandalas that shift and evolve—like fractals, where patterns repeat infinitely. Imagine a mandala that zooms in forever or pulses with neon colors. It’s math meeting mysticism!

Mandalas Beyond the Page

Why stop at paper? Architects weave mandala shapes into buildings—like the circular layouts of ancient temples or modern art installations. Picture a skyscraper with a mandala facade or a garden maze shaped like one. It’s mandalas going 3D.
